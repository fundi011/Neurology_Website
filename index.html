Neurology Department

at the University of Minnesota, Veterinary Medical Center, Small Animal Hospital

Overview

The Neurology Service at the University of Minnesota Veterinary Medical Center provides comprehensive specialty care to canine and feline patients with medical and surgical neurological conditions. These can include a range of disorders affecting the brain, spinal cord, nerves, and/or muscles.
